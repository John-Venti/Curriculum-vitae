2020:
Mathematical Contest in Modeling
--Meritorious Winner
Utilized Cellular Automata Model to build a simulation model of a sand pile by the sea, based on Liquid Bridge physical theory, considering other environment factors such as rain and waves models in the simulation step. The difference between our results and those of the professional engineering simulation software, ANSYS, is extremely small.

China College Students' Mechanical Engineering Innovation Competition
--Provincial Third prize

China Undergraduate Mathematical Contest in Modeling
--Provincial Second Prize
Based on heat transfer theory, developed a parametric reflow soldering mathematical model and corresponding furnace temperature profile for reflow welding. Utilized optimization algorithms to find the model parameters at the optimum quality.

Provincial Student Innovation and Entrepreneurship Training Program
--The Design and Production of Automatic Rescue USV
Led a team to develop an automatic rescue unmanned surface vessel, including mechanical design, electronic and control system development and simulation of the offshore environment. The control algorithm was written in C++, the position information was determined using GPS and inertial guidance, LORA and Bluetooth were used for communication, and ANSYS was used to optimize the flow field of the unmanned ship shape.
